# The HideAway Tour And Travels - Travel Website Specification

## Overview
A comprehensive travel website for The HideAway Tour And Travels company that showcases destinations, manages tour listings, and handles customer bookings and inquiries.

## Core Features

### Home Page
- Brand introduction with company mission and travel experience highlights
- Hero section with compelling travel imagery
- Overview of services and featured destinations
- Call-to-action buttons directing to tours and booking sections

### Gallery
- Photo gallery showcasing beautiful travel destinations
- Images organized by destination or trip type
- High-quality travel photography with captions

### Tours Page
- Complete listing of available trips and tours
- Each tour displays:
  - Destination name and location
  - Trip duration
  - Price information
  - Detailed description of activities and highlights
  - Booking/inquiry button for each tour

### Booking System
- Online booking form for trip reservations
- Inquiry form for custom trip requests
- Form fields include:
  - Customer contact information
  - Selected tour or custom requirements
  - Travel dates and group size
  - Special requests or questions

### Contact Page
- Company contact information (address, phone, email)
- Contact form for general inquiries
- Business hours and location details

## Backend Data Storage
- Tour listings with details (destination, price, duration, description)
- Customer booking requests and inquiries
- Contact form submissions
- Gallery images and metadata

## Backend Operations
- Store and retrieve tour information
- Process booking requests and inquiries
- Manage contact form submissions
- Handle gallery image management

## Design Requirements
- Mobile-friendly responsive design
- Travel-themed visual design
- Easy navigation between sections
- Professional and attractive layout
- Fast loading times for images and content
